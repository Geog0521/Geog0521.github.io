Xinghua Cheng

I am a full-time Research Associate at Dept. of Land Surveying and Geo-Informatics, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University LSGI, where I work on statistical thermodynamics and remote sensing image processing.

At LSGI, I've worked on HKSAR RGC GRF Project.I did my BSc in GIS at Nanjing Normal University and funded by the NSFC Projects. I did my master at the LSGI, The Hong Kong Polytechnic Univ. I am now supvervised by Chair Prof. Zhilin LI and Dr. Yan Wai Yeung.
