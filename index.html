Key theoretic research topics





Spatial-temporal Remote Sensing Image Fusion: How can we evaluate the fused fine-resolution imagery in practical applications (i.e., "Ground Truth" is unavailable)?
Modelling the Land Cover Mapping from Multispectral Remote Sensing Imagery: The land cover mapping can be considered as a kind of irreversible thermodynamic process from numerical data to nominal raster data.
How to model the landscape pattern change in the ecological process? Themodynamic laws and thermodynamic entropy (Boltzmann entropy) can help!!!
How to model the information flow in the downscaling of remotely sensed data? Multiscale information (compositional and configurational) can be well quantified by thermodynamic entropy (Boltzmann entropy)!.
